Codename: Fake MMO

I've been building a Fake MMO on my own - a project meant to simulate a AAA MMORPG in a single-player experience. Playable demo available here!

Return to Portfolio.

Feature Suite

Simulated Multiplayer

Other "players" will join you in adventuring - they will navigate and participate in questing and combat.

You can check how many players are in the world, and what they're doing, by checking the Simulation Stats in the debug window.

Card Combat

Combat within this game takes the form of card battles. Walk up to an enemy to enter combat, and try to defeat them before they can defeat you!

Your starting library is determined by your choices in character creation. Upgrade cards at crafting tables, or unlock new ones in stores.

Advancement and Progression

As you level up, you'll earn Enhancement Points, to be spent within your Enhancement Trees.

Unlock new trees by unlocking achievements or by speaking to certain NPCs.

Hoverboarding

Inspired by Wildstar! Hoverboards are a great way to get around.

Check out this hoverboard test footage!

Achievements

A modular, data driven, lightning-fast achievement system that supports everything from kill counts to exploration milestones.

Achievements are the lifeblood of any MMO, and I spent a long time making this system as usable as possible.

Other Features Include...

  • Locomotion animations for players and NPC players
  • Keymapping using Unity Player Prefs
  • JSON serialization based saving and loading of Scriptable Objects within player save data
  • Automatic map generation based on terrain splatmaps and heights
  • Automatic map notes that update their positions and rotations dynamically
  • Guild Wars 2 inspired Area Objective based questing
  • Guild Wars 2 inspired Map Completion objectives and rewards
  • Custom enemy AI and idle wander behaviors
  • Itemization and equipment
  • Gold and custom currency rewards for detailed trading
  • Game settings such as hiding the UI and setting the game to Full Screen
